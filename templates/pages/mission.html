RRBN Mission

We promise to promote and enhance the image of Radiography profession in Nigeria and in the west African sub-region through: A) Provision of policies for qualitative academic and professional education at undergraduate and graduate levels; B) Constant provision and revision of policy frame works for quality services and ethical standards for practice of the profession; and C) Entrenchment of measures that are capable of building and sustaining the professional pride and ego of the radiographer at the highest level.

Vision

To position Radiography at the top of health profession in Nigeria
