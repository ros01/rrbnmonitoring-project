About RRBN Inspection & Mornitoring

The Radiographers Registration Boadr of Nigeria (RRBN) is a parastatal under the ministry of health.RRBN was established by Decree N0. 42 of 1987, now Cap R1 Laws of the Fedration of Nigeria 2004.RRBN key mandate is to regulate the price of Radiography in Nigeria.This key mandate of the board is cmostly carried out through its Monitoring and Enforcement Department.

About the RRBN Inspection & Mornitoring Portal

The RRBN Inspection & Mornitoring Portal is an Electronic platform for Data Consolidation of all major activities crried out by the Monitoring and Enforcement Department of the RRBN in its bid to uphold the mandate of the Board to regulate the Practice of radiography in Nigeria. RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement Portal provides a repository of easily retrivable data of all hospitals dispensing ionizing radiation, their addresses, list of equipments, radiographers covering the hospital, practice registration/compliance status, registration and renewal module for ease of registrations/renewals as well as inspection module for inspections and scheduling realtime upload of inspection observation/reports.
