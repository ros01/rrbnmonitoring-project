The RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement Portal is an Electronic Platform for Data Consolidation of all major activities carried out by the Monitoring and Enforcement Department of the RRBN in its bid to uphold the mandate of the Board to regulate the Practice of Radiography in Nigeria.
RRBN Monitoring and Enforcement Portal provides a repository of easily retrievable data of all hospitals dispensing ionizing radation, their addresses, list of equipment, radiographers covering the hospital,practice registration/compliance status, registration and renewal module for ease of registrations/renewals as well as an inspections module for inspections scheduling and real time upload of inspection observations/reports.
