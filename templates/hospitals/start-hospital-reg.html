New Hospital Registration - Profile Creation

Notice: This is the first step for New Hospitals/Centres Registration with the Radiographers Registration Board of Nigeria. Kindly go through the guildlines and conditions. Please note that all entry document uploaded at this stage will be vetted before a user account is created for you on the portal in order for you to complete the registration process.

Requirements for Hospitals Registration

  1. Valid certificate of incorporation.

  2. Form C02 showing names and particulars of director(s) and radiographer(s).

  3. Current Radiographer practicing Licence.

  4. Payment of fifty thousand naira (N50,000.00) Registration fee.

Note: Provisional Registration is valid for 2 years.

Registration Process Flow:

  • STEP 1: Submission of basic hospital/centre details, certificate of incorporation, form C02 and radiographer practicing Licence.

  • STEP 2: Verification of submitted documents, upon successful verification hspital/centre is expected to make payment of registration free of N50,000.00 via Remita.

  • STEP 3: Upon succesful payment, hospital is scheduled for inspection.

  • STEP 4: Facility inspection is carried out by RRBN Inspection and monitoring team from the Head Quarters or Zonal offices.

  • STEP 5: Report of facility inspection communicated to hospital with summary of areas of improvement.

  • STEP 6: Hospital issued Provisional Practice permit valid for 2 years.

Note: Upon issue of provisional permit, Hospital/centre is informed of next date of inspection, monioring. This is required of issue of full registration certificate/accreditation for internship.
