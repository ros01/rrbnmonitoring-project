New Hospital Registration - Profile Creation

Notice: This is the first step for New Hospitals/Centres Registration with the Radiographers Registration Board of Nigeria. Kindly go through the guildlines and conditions. Please note that all entry document uploaded at this stage will be vetted before a user account is created for you on the portal in order for you to complete the registration process.

Requirements for Hospitals Registration

1 Valid certificate of incorporation.

2Form C02 showing names and particulars of director(s) and radiographer(s).

3Current Radiographer practicing Licence

4Payment of fifty thousand naira (N50,000.00) Registration fee.

Note: Provisional Registration is valid for 2 years.

Registration Process Flow

1STEP 1: Submission of basic hospital/centre details, certificate of incorporation, form C02 and radiographer practicing Licence.

2STEP 2: Verification of submitted documents, upon successful verification hspital/centre is expected to make payment of registration free of N50,000.00 via Remita.

3STEP 3: Upon succesful payment, hospital is scheduled for inspection

4STEP 4: Facility inspection is carried out by RRBN Inspection and monitoring team from the Head Quarters or Zonal offices.

5STEP 5: Report of facility inspection communicated to hospital with summary of areas of improvement.

6STEP 6: Hospital issued Provisional Practice permit valid for 2 years.

Note: Upon issue of provisional permit, Hospital/centre is informed of next date of inspection, monioring. This is required of issue of full registration certificate/accreditation for internship.
